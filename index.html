PyGeoRS

A QGIS Plugin for Remote sensing Automation

PyGeoRS is a small, free, and open-source QGIS plugin, developed under Python 3, and designed to automate specific remote sensing tasks (PCA, ICA, MNF, BR, Index, etc.) for environmental mapping. It's an effective alternative to similar commercial software, with its more user-friendly interface and reduced calculation time. These features make PyGeoRS particularly suitable for a diverse audience, such as academics, researchers, and technicians, seeking efficient remote sensing solutions.
